Everything a monitor should do

One app. Both phones. Fully featured.

No dedicated hardware to buy and nothing to sign up for — just two Android phones you already own.

📹

Live video & audio

See and hear the baby's room in real time over an encrypted peer-to-peer connection.

🎙️

Two-way talk

Tap to talk and soothe your baby from the other room, straight to the baby phone's speaker.

🎛️

Full remote control

Toggle the baby phone's camera, microphone and flashlight, and flip front/back — without touching it.

🎵

Lullabies & white noise

Play rain, heartbeat and white noise on the baby phone, controlled from the parent side.

🔔

Gentle chime

Ring a soft chime on the baby phone to get attention, without startling anyone.

📶

Works anywhere

Same Wi-Fi connects directly; on mobile data it auto-relays so you can watch from another room or the office.

⏰

Safety reminders

Schedule feed, sleep and medicine reminders that ring a full-screen alarm and escalate until acknowledged.

🔒

App lock

Protect the parent view with a PIN or fingerprint, so only you can open the monitor.

🌙

Runs in the background

Keeps monitoring with the screen off, and the parent can wake a sleeping baby phone remotely.

Set up in under a minute

Three steps, no account

1

Install on two phones

Download the APK on both. Choose Baby on the phone that stays by the crib, Parent on the one you keep with you.

2

Enter the 4-digit code

The baby phone shows a 4-digit code. Type it on the parent phone — that's the whole pairing. No QR scanner, no sign-up.

3

Watch & listen

You're connected. The pairing is remembered and reconnects automatically until you unpair.

Private by design

Your baby's room stays yours.

BabyCam has no backend that touches your video. The stream flows directly between your two phones and is end-to-end encrypted — we can't see it, upload it, or store it.

  • Peer-to-peer video & audio, end-to-end encrypted (DTLS-SRTP)
  • No account, no sign-up, no cloud recording
  • Camera & mic start OFF — the parent turns them on
